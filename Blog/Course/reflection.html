Character Rationale and Reflection

26 June 2020

I am very fortunate to have been able to go on a few trips overseas. I really enjoy travelling and seeing new places and it’s something that I aspire to do more of eventually. I’ve often felt somewhat jealous of, say, people living in Europe because travelling just seems so much more accessible. My parents have often told stories of the travelling they did while living in England until after I was born. I dream of having that sort of experience and it would be ideal if I could have it while still living in South Africa. I also wish for others to be able to go and experience different countries.

My character is living out that dream. In Grace’s reality 10 years from now, the South African economy is thriving and it is much more affordable to travel the world (a bit optimistic for such a short period of time, I know). Grace wants to inspire other South Africans to travel and experience as much of the world as they can. She doesn’t only write about other countries, but she often writes about experiences in South Africa both for locals and for potential visitors. She’s very proud to call South Africa home and, even though she absolutely loves her time overseas, she always looks forward to returning.

I created this character as a way to fantasise a bit about being able to travel a lot from Johannesburg. I felt inspired because I had just booked a trip to Dublin to see my favourite band and to visit family, which I was very excited about and it made me feel like it might be possible for me to travel more in the future. Unfortunately that trip has been postponed, hopefully to next year, which has made writing about a travel blogger a bit bleak. I was almost tempted to change my character but then I would have been under even more pressure. At least this character gives me an outlet to think about adventures I’ve had previously and look forward to possibilities in the future.
